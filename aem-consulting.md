# Adobe Experience Manager consulting

I help teams design, build, and improve Adobe Experience Manager solutions that are easier to deliver, safer to maintain, and less painful to extend.

My work is centered on real delivery problems in AEM projects: component architecture, maintainability, deployment friction, authoring experience, and long-term technical direction.

## Where I can help

If you need support with AEM, I can help with:

* AEM component and template design
* codebase reviews and architecture cleanup
* improving dispatcher, deployment, and release practices
* making authoring workflows simpler and less fragile
* performance and maintainability improvements in existing implementations
* technical guidance for teams delivering AEM-based CMS platforms

## Background

Publicly available profile information and author bios describe me as a software engineer specialized in delivering and maintaining CMS systems based on Adobe Experience Manager, with around 10 years in the industry. My published AEM writing focuses on modularity, architecture, and practical delivery concerns rather than generic platform marketing.

That is the angle I bring to consulting: not just "how to make AEM work", but how to make it workable for a team over time.

## Typical engagement

I am a good fit when your team is dealing with one of these situations:

* an AEM codebase that became slow, messy, or hard to change
* uncertainty around component boundaries and project structure
* difficult authoring experience that creates business friction
* release or quality problems caused by poor engineering practices
* the need for a second technical opinion before making larger platform decisions
